GEM REWARDS FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

How many gems can I earn per week?

Players can earn approximately 4,043 gems per week from all sources: events (500), PvP (~1,850 with default Elite II rank 13 settings), login rewards (1,393), and promo codes (300). Your actual total varies based on PvP league and rank selections.

What is the best way to earn free gems?

Login rewards are the most reliable source at 1,393 gems/week guaranteed. PvP offers the highest potential but varies by league and rank. Events require competitive rankings within the top 5-10%. Promo codes are the easiest — just tap to reveal and redeem 300 gems instantly.

How do PvP league multipliers work?

Each league has its own payout brackets. Higher leagues pay more gems and PvP Currency at every rank. For example, Elite II at rank 13 pays 520 gems in Restricted Arena, while the same rank in Intern pays only 80 gems. PvP Currency rewards follow the same pattern.

How much do login rewards give?

Login rewards total 1,393 gems per week: daily login gives 130/day (910/week), weekly gives 460 (60+400), and monthly gives 90/4 = 23/week. Combined monthly total is approximately 5,572 gems.

What are the active promo codes?

There are 24 active promo codes with rewards including gems and Hero Shop Tickets. Generate a verification code in your game settings (top left LVL), then redeem at redeem.invincible.ubisoft.barcelona. Relaunch the game and rewards appear in a pop up. New codes are released through official game channels and community events.

What events give gem rewards?

Two recurring events offer gem rewards: The Long Haul (300 gems, top 5%) and Earth's Defenders (200 gems, top 10%). Both require active participation and competitive performance. Alliance membership significantly improves your chances of reaching the top thresholds.

What is the demotion zone in PvP?

If your rank reaches 86 or higher during Alliance War, you enter the demotion zone. After the war concludes, players at or above rank 86 risk being demoted to a lower league, which reduces their gem income tier in subsequent seasons.
