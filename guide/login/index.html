Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I miss a day?

Missing a day means losing 130 gems from that day's login reward. Your streak may also reset depending on the game's streak system, meaning you lose progress toward consecutive day bonuses. Consistent daily logging is essential for maximizing login income.

When does the weekly reset happen?

The weekly reset occurs every Monday at server reset time. This resets both the daily login streak and the weekly login bonus. Plan your schedule around Monday to claim all pending rewards before the reset.

Can I claim login rewards on multiple devices?

Yes, as long as your account is linked. Login rewards are tied to your account, not your device. You can log in on any linked device and your rewards progress will be synced. This is useful if you need to claim rewards while away from your primary device.

Are login rewards enough to play for free?

Login rewards alone provide ~1,393 gems per week, which is a solid foundation for free-to-play players. Combined with events (500), PvP (~1,850), and promo codes (300), the total ~4,043 weekly gems are sufficient for regular progression without spending money.
