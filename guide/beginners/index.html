BEGINNER'S GUIDE

Getting Started with Gem Rewards

Your Weekly Gem Income

~3,643 GEMS / WEEK

As a new player, you can earn approximately 3,643 gems per week from all sources. This guide breaks down each source and helps you prioritize your efforts for maximum gem income.

Where Gems Come From

Login Rewards

993 / week

The easiest and most reliable source. Just log in daily, weekly, and monthly. Start here as a new player — 993 guaranteed gems per week requires zero competitive effort.

Event Rewards

500 / week

Requires ranking in the top 5-10% of The Long Haul and Earth's Defenders events. Join an alliance early — it dramatically improves your chances of reaching top tiers.

PvP Rewards

~1,850 / week

Your league and rank determine PvP income. Start with the default Elite II at rank 13 and climb as you improve. All three arenas (Restricted, Open, Alliance War) contribute independently.

Promo Codes

300 / code

Tap to reveal and redeem. Follow official social media channels and community Discord servers to catch new codes as they're released. Current code: 30KGTG.

Beginner Priority Checklist

  1. 1
    Log in daily — set a daily reminder. This is your most reliable gem source at 910/week. Never miss a day.
  2. 2
    Redeem the promo code — 30KGTG gives you 300 instant gems. Tap the code card on the main infographic to reveal and copy.
  3. 3
    Join an alliance — alliance membership unlocks score multipliers for events and enables Multiverse Alliance War participation.
  4. 4
    Participate in events — even if you don't reach top tiers, participating in events earns valuable experience and helps you understand the mechanics.
  5. 5
    Explore PvP arenas — start with Restricted Arena and Open Arena. Your default Elite II league gives ×0.85 multiplier on all gem rewards.

Gem Spending Tips for New Players

  • ▸ Save gems for hero upgrades — investing in a strong core roster pays long-term dividends in all game modes
  • ▸ Avoid impulse chest pulls — premium chests are tempting but often yield poor returns. Save for guaranteed upgrades
  • ▸ Focus on daily operations — completing daily tasks unlocks the full 130 gems/day login reward
  • ▸ Track your income — use the main gem rewards infographic to monitor your weekly totals across all four categories
