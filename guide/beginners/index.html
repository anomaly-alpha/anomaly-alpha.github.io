Gameplay Basics

Invincible Guarding the Globe is an idle squad RPG where you collect heroes from the Invincible universe, build teams, and battle through a brand-new storyline. Here's what every new player needs to know:

Team Composition

Each team can hold up to 5 heroes across three essential roles: Attackers (deal damage), Defenders (absorb damage and protect allies), and Support (heal and buff). A balanced team with all three roles performs best. You can swap heroes between battles, so experiment with different combinations.

Controls & Speed

The game features auto-battle controls that let the AI handle skill usage — great for easier fights and grinding. You can also enable 2x speed to complete battles twice as fast. For tough boss fights, switch to manual control to time your ultimate abilities strategically.

Gear & Artifacts

Equip heroes with gear (weapons, armor, accessories found in battles) to boost stats. Each gear piece has a rarity and role attribute. Artifacts are unique items that grant special abilities — obtained from The Lab and special events. Check and upgrade gear regularly.

Game Modes

Campaign Mode

The main story mode — progress through chapters to unlock new heroes, earn rewards, and experience the original storyline. Chapters get tougher as you advance, requiring stronger teams and better strategies. This is where you'll spend most of your early game time.

GDA Ops

Time-limited battles where you clear waves of enemies for rewards. Your top source of Hero XP and Hero Special XP. Set a team to auto-fight and earn passive XP even while offline. Higher player (Agent) levels unlock better GDA Ops battles with better rewards.

PvP Arenas

Three arenas: Restricted Arena, Open Arena, and Alliance War (Multiverse Arena). Each has 14 leagues with 120 ranks and its own payout table. You earn gems, PvP currency, and hero tickets based on your league and rank. Start at the default Elite II / rank 13 and climb as you improve. See the PvP guide for full league payout tables.

Alliances

Join or create an alliance to unlock cooperative gameplay. Alliances provide score multipliers for events (essential for reaching top tiers), enable Alliance War PvP, and grant Agent XP for account progression. Join one on day one — it dramatically improves your rewards in almost every game mode.
